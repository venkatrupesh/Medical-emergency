Railway Medical Emergency Response System

A dedicated system for reporting medical emergencies during train journeys. Station managers and railway officials can coordinate immediate medical assistance.

For Passengers

If you or a fellow passenger needs medical assistance during your train journey:

  • Click "Report Medical Emergency" above
  • Provide train and location details
  • Medical team will be arranged at nearest station

For Railway Officials

Station managers and authorized personnel can access the dashboard to:

  • View all emergency reports
  • Track train locations
  • Coordinate medical response

Important Information

For Life-Threatening Emergencies: Call emergency helpline 112 or railway helpline 139 immediately. Do not rely solely on this system for critical emergencies.

This system assists in coordinating medical assistance but does not replace emergency medical services. Always call emergency services for immediate life-threatening situations.
